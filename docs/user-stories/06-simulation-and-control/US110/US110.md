# US110 - Incorporate Environmental Influences into the Simulation

## User Story

As a simulation engine, I want to incorporate environmental influences into the simulation so that flight behaviour reflects relevant weather and environmental conditions.

Environmental influences may include wind, temperature, pressure, visibility and other weather-related data. These influences should be applied during simulation execution and may affect aircraft position updates, fuel consumption, safety checks or simulation results.
