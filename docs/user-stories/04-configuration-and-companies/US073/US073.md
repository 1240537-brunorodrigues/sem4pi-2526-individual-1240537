# US073 - Create a Flight Route

## User Story

As an Air Transport Company Collaborator, I want to create a flight route for my company.

A flight route connects an origin airport to a destination airport and belongs to an air transport company. The route must have a unique name composed of the company's two-letter initials followed by up to four digits, such as TP123.
