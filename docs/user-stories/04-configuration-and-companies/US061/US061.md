# US061 - Add a Customer's Collaborator

## User Story

As a Backoffice Operator, I want to register a customer's collaborator.

A customer may be an air transport company or an air control area. The collaborator will also be a user of the system. Thus, each collaborator will be a distinct system user. There is no need to verify that the collaborator's email is in the customer's domain. This could be extremely difficult or even impossible, and many users have emails outside the company's main domain. This must also be achievable by a bootstrap process.
